    Special Events

    General Notes

    1. All Pokémon here have the normal chance to be shiny. There are no shiny locks.

    Gift Pokémon

    Baby Pokémon Egg Gift

    Location: Jubilife City Trainer School
Level: 1
    1. Talk to the Cowgirl in the Jubilife School to receive an Egg.
    2. The Egg will hatch into one of the following Pokémon at random: Pichu, Cleffa, Igglybuff, Togepi, Tyrogue, Smoochum, Elekid, Magby, Azurill, Wynaut, Budew, Chingling, Bonsly, Mime Jr., Happiny, Munchlax, Riolu, Mantyke.
    3. You can save before collecting the Egg and soft reset if you don't like the result.

    #1 Bulbasaur, #4 Charmander, #7 Squirtle

    Location: Jubilife City Pokémon Center
Level: 5
    1. Talk to the Interviewer at the top left.
    2. Answer her questions correctly and win the battle.
    3. Bulbasaur will be the first Pokémon she gives you.
    4. Charmander will be the second Pokémon she gives you.
    5. Squirtle will be the third Pokémon she gives you.
    6. If your party gets full before all three are given, she'll stop and you can talk again to receive the rest.

    #131 Lapras

    Location: Pastoria City
Level: 35
    1. In one of the houses is an old lady. Agree to listen to her story.
    2. She'll give you a Lapras after she finishes.

    #133 Eevee

    Location: Twinleaf Town
Level: 5
    1. Complete the events as far as Lucas/Dawn telling you to speak to your mother.
    2. Speak to your mother and she'll suggest taking Eevee.
    3. Interact with the Poké Ball to get Eevee.

    #137 Porygon

    Location: Eterna Galactic Building
Level: 22
    1. Defeat Jupiter at the Eterna Galactic Building.
    2. Interact with the Poké Ball behind her to get Porygon.

    #138 Omanyte, #140 Kabuto, #142 Aerodactyl, #345 Lileep, #347 Anorith, #408 Cranidos, #410 Shieldon

    Location: Eterna City
Level: 20
    1. Go to the Underground Man's house, near the Pokémon Center in Eterna City.
    2. Talk to the female scientist to receive fossils for all of these Pokémon.
    3. Take the fossils to the museum in Oreburgh City to revive them.

    #152 Chikorita, #155 Cyndaquil, #158 Totodile

    Location: Floaroma Town Pokémon Center
Level: 15
    1. Talk to the Interviewer at the top left.
    2. Answer her questions correctly and win the battle.
    3. Chikorita will be the first Pokémon she gives you.
    4. Cyndaquil will be the second Pokémon she gives you.
    5. Totodile will be the third Pokémon she gives you.
    6. If your party gets full before all three are given, she'll stop and you can talk again to receive the rest.

    #252 Treecko, #255 Torchic, #258 Mudkip

    Location: Oreburgh City Pokémon Center
Level: 10
    1. Talk to the Interviewer at the top left.
    2. Answer her questions correctly and win the battle.
    3. Treecko will be the first Pokémon she gives you.
    4. Torchic will be the second Pokémon she gives you.
    5. Mudkip will be the third Pokémon she gives you.
    6. If your party gets full before all three are given, she'll stop and you can talk again to receive the rest.

    #374 Beldum

    Location: Oreburgh City
Level: 10
    1. Go inside the house near Oreburgh Mine.
    2. Talk to Steven and he'll offer you a Beldum.

    #387 Turtwig, #390 Chimchar, #393 Piplup

    Location: Sandgem Town
Level: 5
    1. Interact with the briefcase at the top right of Rowan's lab.
    2. Rowan will say a few words and then allow you to take out the Poké Balls inside.
    3. Interact with the briefcase again to receive the two Sinnoh starters that you didn't choose.
    4. Turtwig, Chimchar and Piplup will be given in that order, with the one you did select not appearing.

    Special Encounters

    #94 Gengar

    Location: Old Chateau
Level: 20
    1. Talk to the painting with the glowing red ones in one of the rooms at the back of the Old Chateau.
    2. Attempt to leave the room and you'll encounter Gengar.
    3. This event can only be performed once.

    #442 Spiritomb

    Location: Route 209
Level: 30
    1. Get the Odd Keystone from a man on Route 208, hidden in Twinleaf Town or from the Underground.
    2. Talk to the broken stone tower to encounter Spiritomb.

    #444 Gabite

    Location: Wayward Cave
Level: 25
    1. Go through the basement and up the stairs to the small room.
    2. Walk forward and you'll encounter Gabite.
    3. This event can only be performed once.

    #479 Rotom

    Location: Old Chateau
Level: 20
    1. Talk to the TV in one of the rooms at the back of the Old Chateau.
    2. The event is now repeatable and can be done at any time of day.

    Legendary Encounters

    #144 Articuno

    Location: Mt. Coronet (Summit)
Level: 60
    1. Speak to Rowan after the Distortion World events.
    2. Head to the snowy parts of Mt. Coronet. Bring something with Rock Climb.
    3. Just above the spiralling paths entrance is a portal.
    4. Talk to the portal to encounter Articuno.
    5. You can keep battling Articuno until you catch it.

    #145 Zapdos

    Location: Valley Windworks
Level: 60
    1. Speak to Rowan after the Distortion World events.
    2. Head to Valley Windworks. Bring something with Surf.
    3. On an island behind the building is a portal.
    4. Talk to the portal to encounter Zapdos.
    5. You can keep battling Zapdos until you catch it.

    #146 Moltres

    Location: Victory Road
Level: 60
    1. Speak to Rowan after the Distortion World events.
    2. Head to Victory Road. Bring something with Rock Climb.
    3. In a small room accessible from the last big area is a portal.
    4. Talk to the portal to encounter Moltres.
    5. You can keep battling Moltres until you catch it.

    #150 Mewtwo

    Location: Oreburgh Gate
Level: 70
    1. After the credits, a portal will appear in the bottom floor of Oreburgh Gate.
    2. Once you gain access, talk to the nurse in the Battleground to receive the Odd Invitation.
    3. Go to Oreburgh Gate. Bring something with Surf.
    4. In the basement as far as in as you can go with Surf is a portal.
    5. Talk to the portal with the Odd Invitation in your inventory to encounter Mewtwo.
    6. You can keep battling Mewtwo until you catch it.

    #151 Mew

    Location: Route 201
Level: 40
    1. A Shroomish is in the top-left of Route 203 near some trees.
    2. Talk to it with an Oran Berry in your inventory. It'll take it, transform into Mew and disappear.
    3. After seeing the Shroomish part, you'll find a Buneary on Route 209 above a muddy slope.
    4. Talk to it with a Sitrus Berry in your inventory. It'll take it, transform into Mew and disappear.
    5. After seeing the Buneary part, you'll find a Torchic in Pastoria City on the alcove above the lake.
    6. Talk to it with an Old Gateau in your inventory. It'll take it, transform into Mew and disappear.
    7. After seeing the Torchic part, you'll find a Pachirisu on Route 201.
    8. Talk to it and it'll transform into Mew, but stay in place.
    9. Talk to Mew to encounter it.
    10. You can keep battling Mew until you catch it.

    #243 Raikou

    Location: Route 208
Level: 60
    1. Speak to Rowan after the Distortion World events.

    #493 Arceus

    Location: Hall of Origin
Level: 80
    1. After the credits, talk to the old woman in the 'Foreign Building' in Hearthome City to make the Type Master trainers appear.
    2. You need to collect all sixteen Plate items (the Pixie Plate has not been added).
    3. You can get the Plates via the Underground or by defeating the Type Master trainers. The Plates previously in the field have been removed.
    4. Type Master trainers must be challenged with only a single Pokémon on your team that shares the type they specialise in.
    5. Flame Plate: The Fire-type Type Master is found on Route 226, just right of the Survival Area.
    6. Splash Plate: The Water-type Type Master is found on Route 219, just below Sandgem Town.
    7. Zap Plate: The Electric-type Type Master is found in Sunyshore City, inside the lighthouse.
    8. Meadow Plate: The Grass-type Type Master is found in Floaroma Meadow, just right of the house.
    9. Icicle Plate: The Ice-type Type Master is found in Snowpoint City, to the right of the Gym.
    10. Fist Plate: The Fighting-type Type Master is found in Veilstone City, in the house just south of the Gym.
    11. Toxic Plate: The Poison-type Type Master is found in Pastoria City, on the top floor of the Great Marsh lobby.
    12. Earth Plate: The Ground-type Type Master is found in Oreburgh Gate, on the top floor north of the entrances.
    13. Sky Plate: The Flying-type Type Master is found at the Pokémon League, looking out on the right outside the building.
    14. Mind Plate: The Psychic-type Type Master is found in Solaceon Ruins, just inside the entrance.
    15. Insect Plate: The Bug-type Type Master is found on Route 202, just above the Sandgem Town entrance.
    16. Stone Plate: The Rock-type Type Master is found on Route 207, just to the right of the muddy slope.
    17. Spooky Plate: The Ghost-type Type Master is found in the Lost Tower, on the ground floor.
    18. Draco Plate: The Dragon-type Type Master is found in Celestic Town, inside the ruins.
    19. Dread Plate: The Dark-type Type Master is found in the Sendoff Spring, just after the entrance from Route 214.
    20. Iron Plate: The Steel-type Type Master is found on Iron Island, just south of the entrance to the cave.
    21. With all sixteen Plates in your inventory, talk to the old woman in the 'Foreign Building' to get your second task: Complete the Pokédex.
    22. Once your National Pokédex is completed to an extent that you're able to view the diploma in Valor Lakefront (i.e. you have obtained all Pokémon except for Mew, Ho-Oh, Lugia, Celebi, Jirachi, Deoxys, Phione, Manaphy, Darkrai, Shaymin, and Arceus), talk to the old woman again to receive the Azure Flute.
    23. Go to Spear Pillar and stand on the flute symbol just as you leave the cave. You'll be prompted to play the Azure Flute.
    24. Head up the stairs to battle Arceus.
    25. You can fight Arceus repeatedly by playing the Azure Flute on the symbol until you catch it.
