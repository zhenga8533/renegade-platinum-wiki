## Is Redux your project? How has Drayano been involved?

### Project Origins

**Redux is AphexCubed's project.** Work began in early COVID-19 lockdown (~March 2020), with development taking approximately two years.

Redux started as a way to pass the time and learn new skills, but AphexCubed quickly found a knack for ROM hacking. After about a year of development (~40% complete), AphexCubed reached out to Drayano and showed him an early build.

Dray liked what he saw and agreed to help out!

### Division of Labor

=== "AphexCubed (~80%)"

    **Project Lead**

    Responsible for:

    - **Scripting** - Making new events, tweaking and changing old ones
    - **Balancing** - Designing all Pok√©mon movesets and trainer teams, distributing items, modifying moves
    - **Text Changes** - Various dialogue and text updates (if you find spelling mistakes, they're probably his!)
    - **Design** - Writing and designing various new additions to the game
    - **Technical Work** - A fair amount of boring and miscellaneous technical work under the hood

    This is the meat of Redux and makes up the bulk of the project.

=== "Drayano (~15%)"

    **Development Partner** ([@Drayano60](https://twitter.com/Drayano60))

    His presence can be felt throughout the game:

    - **Rewriting** the majority of text in the game (significantly improved Redux quality)
    - **Promotion** of the project, particularly on Twitter
    - **Community Connections** - Putting AphexCubed in touch with various members
    - **Second Opinions** - Balance, text, playtesting feedback

=== "Community (~5%)"

## Can I use Redux as a base for my own projects?

!!! question "Interested in Using Redux?"

    If you'd like to use Redux as a base for your own project, **contact AphexCubed** via:

    - Twitter DM: [@AphexCubed](https://twitter.com/AphexCubed)
    - Discord

    AphexCubed will be happy to sort something out with you, and will also ask if Koji and Dray are happy with the arrangement.

!!! warning "Technical Considerations"

    You might encounter significant issues trying to edit the new events and scripts in Redux.

    Get in touch with AphexCubed for technical assistance!
