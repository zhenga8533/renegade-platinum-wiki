## :material-information: Effect

!!! info "Description"

    Inflicts regular damage. Has a 30% chance to lower the target’s accuracy by one stage.

!!! tip "Quick Summary"

    Has a 30% chance to lower the target’s accuracy by one stage.

## :material-book-open: In-Game Description

!!! quote "Platinum"

    The user attacks by shooting out muddy water. It may also lower the foe’s accuracy.
