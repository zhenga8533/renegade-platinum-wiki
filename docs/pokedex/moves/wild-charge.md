## :material-information: Effect

!!! info "Description"

    Inflicts regular damage. User takes 1/4 the damage it inflicts in recoil.

!!! tip "Quick Summary"

    User receives 1/4 the damage it inflicts in recoil.

!!! quote "Platinum"
