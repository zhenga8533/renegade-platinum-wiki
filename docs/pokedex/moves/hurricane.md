## :material-information: Effect

!!! info "Description"

    Inflicts regular damage. Has a 30% chance to confuse the target. This move can hit Pokémon under the effect of bounce, fly, or sky drop. During rain dance, this move has 100% accuracy. During sunny day, this move has 50% accuracy.

!!! tip "Quick Summary"

    Has a 30% chance to confuse the target.
