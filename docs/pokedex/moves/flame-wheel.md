## :material-information: Effect

!!! info "Description"

    Inflicts regular damage. Has a 10% chance to burn the target. Frozen Pokémon may use this move, in which case they will thaw.

!!! tip "Quick Summary"

    Has a 10% chance to burn the target. Lets frozen Pokémon thaw themselves.

## :material-book-open: In-Game Description

!!! quote "Platinum"

    The user cloaks itself in fire and charges at the foe. It may also leave the target with a burn.
