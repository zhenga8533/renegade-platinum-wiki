## :material-information: Effect

!!! info "Description"

    Deals regular damage. Drains 75% of the damage inflicted to heal the user.

!!! tip "Quick Summary"

    Drains 75% of the damage inflicted to heal the user.

## :material-book-open: In-Game Description

!!! quote "Platinum"

    The user steals the target’s HP with a kiss. The user’s HP is restored by over half of the damage taken by the target.
