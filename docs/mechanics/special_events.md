# Special Events

---

## General Notes

1. All Pokémon here have the normal chance to be shiny. There are no shiny locks.

---

## Gift Pokémon

**Baby Pokémon Egg Gift**

```
Location: Jubilife City Trainer School
Level: 1
```

1. Talk to the Cowgirl in the Jubilife School to receive an Egg.
2. The Egg will hatch into one of the following Pokémon at random: Pichu, Cleffa, Igglybuff, Togepi, Tyrogue, Smoochum, Elekid, Magby, Azurill, Wynaut, Budew, Chingling, Bonsly, Mime Jr., Happiny, Munchlax, Riolu, Mantyke.
3. You can save before collecting the Egg and soft reset if you don't like the result.

**#1 Bulbasaur, #4 Charmander, #7 Squirtle**

```
Location: Jubilife City Pokémon Center
Level: 5
```

1. Talk to the Interviewer at the top left.
2. Answer her questions correctly and win the battle.
3. Bulbasaur will be the first Pokémon she gives you.
4. Charmander will be the second Pokémon she gives you.
5. Squirtle will be the third Pokémon she gives you.
6. If your party gets full before all three are given, she'll stop and you can talk again to receive the rest.

**#131 Lapras**

```
Location: Pastoria City
Level: 35
```

1. In one of the houses is an old lady. Agree to listen to her story.
2. She'll give you a Lapras after she finishes.

Level: 5

2. Speak to your mother and she'll suggest taking Eevee.

**#137 Porygon**

**#493 Arceus**

```
Location: Hall of Origin
Level: 80
```

1. After the credits, talk to the old woman in the 'Foreign Building' in Hearthome City to make the Type Master trainers appear.
2. You need to collect all sixteen Plate items (the Pixie Plate has not been added).
3. You can get the Plates via the Underground or by defeating the Type Master trainers. The Plates previously in the field have been removed.
4. Type Master trainers must be challenged with only a single Pokémon on your team that shares the type they specialise in.
5. Flame Plate: The Fire-type Type Master is found on Route 226, just right of the Survival Area.
6. Splash Plate: The Water-type Type Master is found on Route 219, just below Sandgem Town.
7. Zap Plate: The Electric-type Type Master is found in Sunyshore City, inside the lighthouse.
8. Meadow Plate: The Grass-type Type Master is found in Floaroma Meadow, just right of the house.
9. Icicle Plate: The Ice-type Type Master is found in Snowpoint City, to the right of the Gym.
10. Fist Plate: The Fighting-type Type Master is found in Veilstone City, in the house just south of the Gym.
11. Toxic Plate: The Poison-type Type Master is found in Pastoria City, on the top floor of the Great Marsh lobby.
12. Earth Plate: The Ground-type Type Master is found in Oreburgh Gate, on the top floor north of the entrances.
13. Sky Plate: The Flying-type Type Master is found at the Pokémon League, looking out on the right outside the building.
14. Mind Plate: The Psychic-type Type Master is found in Solaceon Ruins, just inside the entrance.
15. Insect Plate: The Bug-type Type Master is found on Route 202, just above the Sandgem Town entrance.
16. Stone Plate: The Rock-type Type Master is found on Route 207, just to the right of the muddy slope.
17. Spooky Plate: The Ghost-type Type Master is found in the Lost Tower, on the ground floor.
18. Draco Plate: The Dragon-type Type Master is found in Celestic Town, inside the ruins.
19. Dread Plate: The Dark-type Type Master is found in the Sendoff Spring, just after the entrance from Route 214.
20. Iron Plate: The Steel-type Type Master is found on Iron Island, just south of the entrance to the cave.
21. With all sixteen Plates in your inventory, talk to the old woman in the 'Foreign Building' to get your second task: Complete the Pokédex.
22. Once your National Pokédex is completed to an extent that you're able to view the diploma in Valor Lakefront (i.e. you have obtained all Pokémon except for Mew, Ho-Oh, Lugia, Celebi, Jirachi, Deoxys, Phione, Manaphy, Darkrai, Shaymin, and Arceus), talk to the old woman again to receive the Azure Flute.
23. Go to Spear Pillar and stand on the flute symbol just as you leave the cave. You'll be prompted to play the Azure Flute.
24. Head up the stairs to battle Arceus.
25. You can fight Arceus repeatedly by playing the Azure Flute on the symbol until you catch it.
